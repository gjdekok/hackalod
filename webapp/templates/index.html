ReLinking Identities

The rich history of Suriname is hidden in a wide range of sources. This is especially true for enslaved people who are obscured from view to the casual observer. By combining historical sources, we can rediscover who these founders of Suriname were, where they lived, and what their neighbourhood looked like.

Explore your history at ReLinking Identities.
